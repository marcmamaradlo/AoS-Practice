About me

I learned photography at a very young age. back then film was the norm.

I found my passion with portrait photography.

Age 23 when i had my first mirrorless camera. since then, nothing has stopped me to take pictures of food, lanscapes, portraits, and creative shots.

Now it's my time to show you how i see the world with my camera.
